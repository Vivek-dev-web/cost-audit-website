Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

Jitin Sharma & Co, Cost Accountants ("the Firm", "we", "us") is committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website www.jitinsharmaco.in or engage with our services.

1. Information We Collect

a) Information You Provide to Us

  • Name, company name, designation
  • Email address and telephone number
  • Business information shared during consultations
  • Messages submitted through our contact form

b) Automatically Collected Information

  • IP address, browser type, operating system
  • Pages visited, time spent, referral URLs
  • Cookies and usage data (see Section 6)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your enquiries and provide professional services
  • Send you relevant updates, newsletters, and regulatory alerts (with your consent)
  • Improve our website and service offerings
  • Comply with legal and professional obligations
  • Prevent fraud and ensure the security of our systems

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information with:

  • Service providers: Website hosting, email service providers — under strict data processing agreements
  • Professional associates: Only when essential for service delivery, and only with your consent
  • Legal authorities: When required by law, court order, or regulatory requirement

4. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide services and comply with legal obligations. Client engagement records are retained for a minimum of 8 years in accordance with ICMAI professional standards and applicable law. Website enquiry data is retained for 3 years.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with the appropriate data protection authority

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@jitinsharmaco.in.

6. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience. We use:

  • Essential cookies: Required for the website to function properly
  • Analytics cookies: Google Analytics 4 — to understand how visitors use our website (anonymised data)
  • Preference cookies: To remember your cookie consent choice

You can control cookie preferences through our cookie consent banner or your browser settings.

7. Third-Party Links

Our website contains links to third-party websites (ICMAI, MCA, GST Portal, etc.). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites and recommend reviewing their individual privacy policies.

8. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorised access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. Our website uses SSL/TLS encryption (HTTPS).

9. Applicable Law

This Privacy Policy is governed by the Information Technology Act 2000, the Information Technology (Reasonable Security Practices and Procedures and Sensitive Personal Data or Information) Rules 2011, and the Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023 (once effective) of India.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of our website after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
