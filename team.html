Partners & Senior Professionals

Er. Vishal Sharma
FCMA · Ph.D.

Er. Vishal Sharma

Founder & Proprietor · Registered Cost Auditor

Qualifications: FCMA (ICMAI F-2847) | Ph.D. in Cost Management, University of Delhi | B.Com (Hons.)

Er. Sharma has over 31 years of experience in statutory cost audit, cost records maintenance, management accounting, and forensic costing. He has personally conducted cost audits in the Steel, Cement, Power, Pharmaceuticals, Sugar, Fertilisers, and Textiles sectors.

He is a registered Cost Auditor under the Companies Act 2013, a registered Valuation Professional under IBC, and has served on the ICMAI Northern India Regional Council. He is a recognised resource person for ICMAI training programmes and a regular speaker at FICCI and CII seminars on cost audit methodology.

CMA Priya Mehta
ACMA · MBA

CMA Priya Mehta

Senior Partner · GST & Indirect Tax Head

Qualifications: ACMA (ICMAI) | MBA (Finance), XLRI Jamshedpur | B.Sc. (Statistics)

CMA Priya Mehta leads the GST compliance, audit, and litigation practice at Sharmaji & Associates. CA Rajiv Sharma

CA Rajiv Sharma

Associate Partner · Direct Tax & Statutory Audit

Qualifications: FCA (ICAI) | DISA (ICAI) | B.Com (Hons.), Delhi University

CA Rajiv Sharma brings 18 years of experience in direct tax advisory, income tax assessments, TDS compliance, and statutory audit. He heads the income tax practice and works closely with the cost audit team to ensure cross-jurisdictional compliance for manufacturing clients.
