Income Tax Services

End-to-end income tax compliance, advisory, and representation for corporates, MSMEs, and individuals across India.

Corporate ITR Filing

Preparation and e-filing of Income Tax Returns (ITR-6, ITR-7) for companies, along with Form 3CA-3CD tax audit report.

Individual & Professional Tax

ITR filing for directors, partners, HNIs, and salaried individuals. HRA, LTA, 80C deduction optimisation.

Assessment & Scrutiny

Handling of income tax scrutiny assessments, faceless assessments, and appeals before CIT(A), ITAT, and High Court.

TDS Compliance

TDS deduction, deposit, quarterly return filing (24Q, 26Q, 27Q), and issuance of Form 16/16A. TDS reconciliation and rectifications.

Transfer Pricing

Benchmarking study, documentation as per Section 92D, Form 3CEB certification, and APA application assistance.

Tax Planning & Advisory

Advance tax computation, tax-efficient structuring, Section 80IC/80IB/80IBA benefits, and MAT/AMT advisory.

Tax Audit (Section 44AB)

Preparation and filing of Tax Audit Report in Form 3CA-3CD for companies with turnover above the threshold.

International Tax

DTAA applicability, withholding tax certificates (15CA/15CB), FEMA compliance for cross-border transactions.

Tax Compliance, Simplified

From ITR filing to ITAT representation — our CA team manages your complete income tax lifecycle.
