Specialised Services

Advanced cost advisory beyond compliance — transfer pricing, forensic audit, business valuation, anti-dumping, and more.

Transfer Pricing

Benchmarking study, comparables research, documentation (Section 92D), Form 3CEB certification, and APA application assistance.

Inventory & Stock Audit

Physical verification, dead stock identification, inventory valuation, ABC analysis, and reconciliation with ERP records.

Anti-Dumping Costing

Cost of production data for DGTR investigations, safeguard duty proceedings, and countervailing duty cases before the Ministry of Commerce.

Cost Reduction & Profit Improvement

Identification of cost reduction opportunities across procurement, production, and overheads. Profit improvement roadmap with quantified savings targets.

Forensic Audit

Investigation of suspected fraud, misappropriation, and financial irregularities. Evidence-based reports suitable for legal proceedings.

Business Valuation

Fair value and enterprise value assessments for M&A, shareholder disputes, IBC insolvency proceedings, and ESOP valuation.

TEV Studies

Techno-Economic Viability (TEV) studies for bank financing of new manufacturing projects, capacity expansions, and restructuring.

Bank Audit

Concurrent audit, revenue audit, and stock audit for banks and NBFCs — covering manufacturing company loan accounts.

Local Content Certificate (Make in India)

Certification of local content percentage for government procurement, defence manufacturing, and PLI scheme compliance.

SOX Compliance

Sarbanes-Oxley compliance for Indian subsidiaries of US-listed companies — Section 302/404 documentation and testing.

IBC / Insolvency Services

Registered Valuation Professional under IBBI — asset valuation, liquidation value assessments, and support to Resolution Professionals.

Cooperative Bank Certification

Document certification and audit support for cooperative banks and credit societies under the Maharashtra Co-operative Societies Act and other state Acts.
