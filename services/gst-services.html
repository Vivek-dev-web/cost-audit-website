GST Services

End-to-end GST compliance — registration, returns, audit, reconciliation, and litigation support across India.

GST Registration

New GST registrations, amendments, additional place of business registration, and cancellations. PAN-India registration in all states.

GST Return Filing

Monthly (GSTR-1, 3B) and quarterly return filing, GSTR-9 annual return, GSTR-9C reconciliation statement preparation and filing.

GST Audit (Sec. 65/66)

Assistance in GST audit by tax authorities (Section 65) and special audit (Section 66). Preparation of audit response and reconciliation.

ITC Reconciliation

Reconciliation of Input Tax Credit between GSTR-2A/2B and books of accounts. Identification of mismatches and rectification strategies.

GST Litigation & Representation

Reply to show cause notices, adjudication hearings, appeals before Appellate Authority, GST Tribunal, and High Court representation.

GST Health Check / Due Diligence

Comprehensive review of past GST compliance to identify exposure, missed ITC, incorrect rate applications, and filing discrepancies.

E-Way Bill Management

E-way bill generation, extension, cancellation management and training for your logistics and dispatch teams.

GST Advisory

Advance rulings, GST rate classification opinions, supply vs. non-supply determinations, place of supply advisory, and valuation guidance.
