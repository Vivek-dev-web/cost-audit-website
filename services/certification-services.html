Detailed Page

Local Content Certificate (Make in India)

Certification of local content percentage for government procurement policies, DPIIT PLI schemes, and defence manufacturing.

Duty-Paid Materials Certificate

Certification of duty-paid raw material cost for input credit claims, customs assessments, and export benefit applications.

Cost of Production Certificate (General)

General purpose cost of production certificates for contracts, tenders, court proceedings, or banking purposes.

Cooperative Bank Document Certification

Certification of financial documents, fund utilisation certificates, and loan documents for cooperative banks.

Export Incentive Certificates

Cost-related certifications for RoDTEP, SEIS, advance authorization, and EPCG scheme compliance.

Repairs & Maintenance Certificates

Certification of R&M expenses for insurance claims, lease agreements, and regulatory compliance purposes.

Arm's Length Price Certificate

Independent certification of arm's length pricing for related party transactions in inter-company agreements.

All certificates carry UDIN — Unique Document Identification Number generated from ICMAI's portal, ensuring authenticity and preventing misuse. Certificates are issued only by CMA Dr. Suresh K. Mehta (FCMA, ICMAI F-2847) or other authorised members of our team.
