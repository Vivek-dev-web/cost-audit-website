Our Story

Founded on a Vision of Cost Transparency

In 1993, Er. Vishal Sharma — a young Fellow Member of the Institute of Cost and Works Accountants of India (now ICMAI) — established Sharmaji & Associates in New Delhi with a singular vision: to make cost audit and management accounting accessible, rigorous, and impactful for Indian manufacturers.

Over three decades, the firm has grown from a single-proprietor practice serving small manufacturers in Delhi NCR to a pan-India firm with offices in Mumbai, Pune, Jaipur, and Faridabad, serving 500+ clients ranging from MSMEs to publicly listed companies, PSUs, and joint ventures.

The firm has filed over 1,200 cost audit reports with the Ministry of Corporate Affairs, advised on complex cost structures across 15+ regulated industry sectors, and helped clients avoid regulatory penalties totaling several crores through proactive compliance management.

Founder & Proprietor

Er. Vishal Sharma, FCMA

Dr. Vishal Sharma is the Founder and Proprietor of Sharmaji & Associates, Cost Accountants. A Fellow Member of the Institute of Cost Accountants of India (ICMAI Membership No. F-2847) and a holder of a Doctorate in Cost Management from the University of Delhi, Er. Sharma brings a rare combination of academic rigour and industry-hardened practical experience to every engagement.

He has personally conducted cost audits in the Steel, Cement, Power, Pharmaceuticals, Sugar, and Fertiliser sectors, and has represented clients before the Cost Audit Branch of the Ministry of Corporate Affairs. He has also served on the ICMAI Northern India Regional Council and contributed to the development of Cost Accounting Standards (CAS) as a subject matter expert.

Er. Sharma is a regular speaker at industry conferences, ICMAI seminars, and FICCI/CII events on topics including cost audit methodology, CAS applicability, and forensic cost analysis.

Registered Cost Auditor — Authorised to conduct statutory cost audits under the Companies (Cost Records and Audit) Rules 2014. All audit reports are signed by Er. Sharma under the firm's stamp.

Our Journey

Three Decades of Growth & Impact

1993

Founding of the Firm

Established in New Delhi by CMA Vishal Sharma. Initial focus on cost records maintenance for SMEs in Delhi NCR's manufacturing belt.

1997

First Statutory Cost Audit

Appointed as Cost Auditor for a steel rolling mill in Faridabad — first statutory cost audit assignment under the Companies (Cost Audit Report) Rules.

2003

Mumbai Branch Opens

Expansion into Western India with the opening of the Mumbai office to serve Pharmaceutical and Chemical sector clients.

2008

PSU & Government Mandates

Appointment as Cost Auditor for a Central Government PSU marks entry into the public sector. Pune office established.

2014

New Rules Transition

Successfully transitioned all clients to the Companies (Cost Records and Audit) Rules 2014 framework with XBRL-based MCA filings.

2017

GST Practice Launched

Established a dedicated GST compliance and advisory practice ahead of the GST rollout, serving 100+ manufacturer clients.

2021

IBC & Valuation Practice

Registered as Valuation Professional under IBC. Jaipur and Faridabad offices opened to serve Rajasthan and Haryana markets.

2025

500+ Clients · 5 Offices · 1,200+ Audits

Today, Sharmaji & Associates is a 12-member firm serving clients across 15+ industrial sectors with a reputation for zero-error compliance.
