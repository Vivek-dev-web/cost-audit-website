About Our Firm

Precision in Compliance, Excellence in Advisory

Founded in 2018, Jitin Sharma & Co, Cost Accountants is one of India's most respected Cost Audit & Management Accounting firms. Led by CMA Jitin Sharma, FCMA — a Fellow Member of the Institute of Cost Accountants of India (ICMAI) — the firm brings over three decades of statutory cost audit expertise to companies across 15+ industrial sectors.

We serve listed companies, PSUs, MSMEs, and multinational entities, helping them achieve full compliance with the Companies (Cost Records and Audit) Rules 2014, GST Act, and Cost Accounting Standards (CAS 1–24). Our multi-disciplinary team of CMAs, CAs, CSs, and Advocates provides end-to-end advisory from costing system design to MCA XBRL filing.
